Misspent Youth

10 Years as a Lawn Dart

Where I'm from, upon graduation from High School, you had five career choices, Army, Navy, Marines, Air Force, Coast Guard....who dat? I joined the Army because they promised I'd get the opportunity to go to Airborne School. C-130 rolling down the strip...boy that song just makes me sick!

2nd Ranger BN

Why did I do this?

After Jump School, I volunteer to attend RIP or Ranger Indoctrination Program. While, I'm proud of my 12 months in the 2nd Battalion, it was a grind. I had alot more fun in the 82nd Airborne Div. I speak to younger guys who went to Ranger School and they say its too easy. Class 12/89 Winter Ranger
